What Makes a Hobby Healthy?

A healthy hobby is one that supports your mental, emotional, or physical well-being. It can be something creative like painting, active like cycling, or mindful like yoga. The key is that it makes you feel fulfilled and refreshed.

How to Find the Right Hobby

Start by identifying activities that make you lose track of time or bring you peace. Experiment with different hobbies until you find one that fits your personality and lifestyle. Remember, hobbies are personal and meant to bring joy!
